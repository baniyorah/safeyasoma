How was Amber Wheeler went Missing?

Amber Wheeler, a nurse from Detroit, Michigan, went missing on December 18, 2021, under mysterious circumstances. She was last seen in the vicinity of E. Outer Drive and Mound Road and was on her way to work, but never arrived at her job site.

Her family and friends were immediately concerned when they failed to make contact with her, especially since it was reported that she was an extremely punctual and reliable person.

The alarm was raised further when her 2017 black Honda Civic was found abandoned on the side of the road with its engine still running.

Despite extensive investigations by the police department and a concerted effort by the community to locate her, including a social media campaign, no substantial leads have yet been found.

Amber Wheeler’s disappearance remains a mystery, and her family and friends continue to hold out hope for her safe return home.
