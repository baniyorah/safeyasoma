Who is Diego Tinoco?

Diego Tinoco is an American Actor. Diego is famous for Teen Wolf (TV Series) 2016 in the role of Mateo in On My Block (TV Series) from 2018-2021, and R#J (Drama) as Tybalt in 2021.

As of 2022, he made his appearance in Bad Hombres (Crime Thriller) which is in Post-Production.

Who are the parents of Diego Tinoco?

Diego was born on 25th November 1997 in Anaheim, California, United States. He is the son of Borrelia and Quito. Besides that, he has an older brother, Luigi Tinoco.

Moreover, Tinoco holds American nationality meanwhile, he belongs to a mixed (Mexican and Colombian) ethnicity. Moving towards his educational background, he attended El Segundo High School.

Diego Tinoco: Career

Diego Tinoco debuted as Marco in Drizzle of Hope (Short) in 2015. In the following year, he played the role of Mateo in Teen Wolf (TV Series). Similarly, he played the role of Diego in Welcome to Valhalla (Short) in 2017.

Besides that, Tinoco was self-cast in Noches Con Plataniti (TV Series) in 2018. In the next year, he made his self appearance in Red Carpet Report (TV Series Short). In 2020, he was seen in TikTok Challenge Challenge (TV Series).

Moreover, Diego was portrayed in R#J (Drama) in 2021. Furthermore, he will be seen as A Mysterious Assassin in Knights of the Zodiac which is in Post-Production in 2023. In addition, he will be seen as Bineshii in Mourning War which is in Pre-Production.
