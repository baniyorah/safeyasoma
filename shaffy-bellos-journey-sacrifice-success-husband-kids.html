Shaffy Bello is a renowned Nigerian actress, singer, and producer.

The attractive actress was born in 1967 in Lagos and rose to stardom after starring in the hit film ‘Domitilla’ in 1997.

Since then, she has been featured in numerous films and TV shows over the years, cementing her standing as one of Nigeria’s most popular stars.

But beyond being in the limelight, the gifted star has also managed to raise a loving family with her husband, Mr. Akinrimisi, and their two children. But it wasn’t wall easy for the actress.

Shaffy Bello Candidly Shares Her Struggles Balancing Family And Career

First, here’s the love story.  It all began in the early 90s when Shaffy was already starring in many films across the country.

She came across her future husband, Mr. Akinrimisi, at a showbiz party at a friend’s house and immediately fell for him. Later, the pair happily married.

Nigerian actress Shaffy Bello has opened up about her life, marriage, kids, and career in an interview with Journalist Trude Jidongwo.

In the interview, Shaffy Bello said that she wanted to spread her wings and pursue her acting career in Nigeria, which meant leaving her husband and kids behind in the US.

Bello said that this decision ultimately affected her family, and she paid the price of having a connection with her children.

The actress also mentioned that she would stay in her room sometimes and cry, thinking about what she was doing, but she knew that it was a sacrifice she had to make for her career.

The 59-year-old award-winning actress also spoke about her marriage, revealing that she got divorced five years ago.

Shaffy Bello said that her marriage ended because of the distance, and she wanted more. Bello explained that she would always travel back and forth between the US and Nigeria.

Finally, the Nigerian star also mentioned that if given the opportunity, she would try her hands at marriage again.

Still, with the right person who can double what she can do, she is happy with her current life.
