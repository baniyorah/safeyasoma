Who is Brianna Hildebrand?

Brianna Hildebrand is an American actress. She is known for appearing in the web series Annie Undocumented, and as Negasonic Teenage Warhead in the 2016 film Deadpool.

Brianna Hildebrand: Birth Facts, Family, and Childhood

Hildebrand was born Brianna Caitlin Hildebrand on 14 August 1996, in College Station, Texas, the United States of America.

Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is mixed(German, English, Irish, and Mexican).

Her mother’s name is Veronica and her father’s name is Caleb Hildebrand. There is no information on her siblings.

Brianna Hildebrand: Professional Life and Career

Hildebrand’s first major credit was in 2014 in the television series Annie Undocumented. It also marked the debut of her acting career. In that TV series, she played the role of Jen.

The series was named the best web series at the 2014 New York TV Festival. It was created by Daniel Hsia, Elaine Low, and Brian Yang. She next appeared in the movie Prism in 2015 as Julia.

The same year, she also appeared in the short movie, The Voice Inside, as Grace. Hildebrand also went on to make her production debut in the same year from The Voice Inside.

She rocketed to fame after being cast alongside Ryan Reynolds in the 2016 Marvel film Deadpool.

In the movie, she portrayed the comic book character of Negasonic Teenage Warhead. The film was shot in Vancouver in April 2015 and released on February 12, 2016.

In the same year, she was also cast in the film The First Girl I Loved, as Sasha Basanez.

Briana also appeared as Negasonic Teenage Warhead in the video documentary entitled, Deadpool: From Comics to Screen… to Screen in 2016. In 2017, she appeared as Sadie Cunningham in Tragedy Girls.

Hildebrand is also set to reprise her role as Negasonic Teenage Warhead in the sequel to Deadpool, Deadpool 2. The movie is slated to be released in mid-2018.
